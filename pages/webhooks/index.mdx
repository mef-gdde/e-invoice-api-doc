# Webhook
You can the document notification update by setting webhook url on [setting](https://caminv-dev-merc.mef.gov.kh/setting) with tab Credential.

This webhook URL use for the updating from Caminv such as **Invoice Received** and **Invoice Sent**
- **Invoice Sent**: This Event will send to supplier after the invoice attest and store in blockchain.
- **Invoice Received**: This Event will send to customer after the invoice attest and store in blockchain.

## What happen when Caminv can't send the invoice to destination?
Caminv will retry triggered again and again after having failed transaction sending to supplier/customer.
