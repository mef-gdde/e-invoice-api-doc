# UBL Credit Note

The table below presents the essential fields required for a UBL (Universal Business Language) credit note. These fields conform to the structure defined in UBL 2.1 Credit Note. It provides details such as occurrence, examples, and definitions of each field. Only relevant and mandatory fields for the General Department of Taxation (GDT), as well as commonly used fields, are included.

Ensure that when creating and submitting your document, the fields are listed in the exact sequential order as below.

Reference:
* [UBL 2.1 Credit Note Schema](https://docs.oasis-open.org/ubl/os-UBL-2.1/xsd/maindoc/UBL-CreditNote-2.1.xsd)
* [UBL 2.1 Common Schema](https://docs.oasis-open.org/ubl/os-UBL-2.1/xsd/common/) 
* [UBL 2.1 Credit Note Documentation](http://www.datypic.com/sc/ubl21/e-ns23_CreditNote.html) 

## Core Invoice Fields Definition
| Data Field            | Occurrence |                        Definition |
|-----------------------|:------------:|-----------------------------------|
|[cbc:ID](./credit-note/id.mdx) |	[1..1] |Unique identifier for the invoice.|
|[cbc:IssueDate](./credit-note/issue-date.mdx) |	[1..1] |Invoice issue date.
|[cbc:Note](./debit-note/note.mdx) | [1..1] |   Contain reasons for Credit Note and Debit Note.
|[cbc:BillingReference](./credit-note/billing-reference.mdx) | [1..1] | A reference to a billing document associated with this document.
|[cac:AdditionalDocumentReference](./credit-note/additional-document-reference) | [0..n] |  A reference to an additional document associated with this document.  
|[cbc:DocumentCurrencyCode](./credit-note/document-currency-code.mdx) |	[1..1]	|The currency in which the invoice is denominated.
|[cac:AccountingSupplierParty](./credit-note/accounting-supplier-party.mdx) |	[1..1] |	Party supplying the goods or services in the invoice.
|[cac:AccountingCustomerParty](./credit-note/accounting-customer-party.mdx) |	[1..1] |	Party to whom the goods or services are supplied.
|[cac:TaxExchangeRate](./credit-note/tax-exchange-rate.mdx) |	[0..1] |	Exchange rate applied for tax calculation in invoices involving conversion, mandatory if document currency is not Khmer Riel(KHR).
|[cac:AllowanceCharge](./credit-note/allowance-charge.mdx) |	[1..n] |	A discount or charge that applies to the invoice on document level.
|[cac:TaxTotal](./credit-note/tax-total.mdx) |	[1..1] |	Total tax amount for each specific tax.
|[cac:LegalMonetaryTotal](./credit-note/legal-monetary-total.mdx) | [1..1] |	Total amounts for the invoice.
|[cac:CreditNoteLine](./credit-note/credit-note-line.mdx) |	[1..n] |	Each individual line item of the invoice.
