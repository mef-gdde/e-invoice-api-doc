# UBL Credit Note
The following table outlines the fields included in a UBL (Universal Business Language) credit note, their occurrence, their examples, and their definitions.
## Core Invoice Fields Definition
| Data Field            | Occurrence |                        Definition |
|-----------------------|:------------:|-----------------------------------|
|[cbc:ID](./invoice/id.mdx) |	[1..1] |Unique identifier for the invoice.|
|[cbc:IssueDate](./invoice/issue-date.mdx) |	[1..1] |Invoice issue date.
|[cbc:DueDate](./invoice/due-date.mdx) |	[0..1] |  Free-form text pertinent to this document, conveying information that is not contained explicitly in other structures.
|[cbc:InvoiceTypeCode](./invoice/invoice-type-code.mdx) | [0..1] |   A code signifying the type of the Invoice.
|[cbc:DocumentCurrencyCode](./invoice/document-currency-code.mdx) |	[1..1]	|The currency in which the invoice is denominated.
|[cac:AccountingSupplierParty](./invoice/accounting-supplier-party.mdx) |	[1..1] |	Party supplying the goods or services in the invoice.
|[cac:AccountingCustomerParty](./invoice/accounting-customer-party.mdx) |	[1..1] |	Party to whom the goods or services are supplied.
|[cac:TaxExchangeRate](./invoice/tax-exchange-rate.mdx) |	[0..1] |	Exchange rate applied for tax calculation in invoices involving conversion, mandatory if document currency is not Khmer Riel(KHR).
|[cac:AllowanceCharge](./invoice/allowance-charge.mdx) |	[1..n] |	A discount or charge that applies to the invoice on document level.
|[cac:TaxTotal](./invoice/tax-total.mdx) |	[1..1] |	Total tax amount for each specific tax.
|[cac:LegalMonetaryTotal](./invoice/legal-monetary-total.mdx) | [1..1] |	Total amounts for the invoice.
|[cac:CreditNoteLine](./invoice/invoice-line.mdx) |	[1..n] |	Each individual line item of the invoice.
