# cac:AllowanceCharge
The conditions and amounts related to an allowance or charge. An "allowance" typically denotes a discount, while a "charge" implies an additional fee.

## About
* Occurrence: [0..1]
* Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonAggregateComponents-2

## Child elements

| Data Field                                        | Occurrence | Definition |
|---------------------------------------------------|:----------:|------------|
| [cbc:ChargeIndicator](./allowance-charge/charge-indicator.mdx)      |   [1..1]   | An indicator that this AllowanceCharge describes a charge (true) or a discount (false). |
| [cbc:AllowanceChargeReason](./allowance-charge/allowance-charge-reason.mdx)         | [0..*] | The reason for this allowance or charge. |
| [cbc:Amount](./allowance-charge/amount.mdx)                                       | [1..1] | The monetary amount of this allowance or charge to be applied. |
| [cac:TaxTotal](./allowance-charge/tax-total.mdx)                                   | [0..1] | The total of all the taxes applicable to this allowance or charge. |

## Example
```xml
<cac:AllowanceCharge>
    <cbc:ChargeIndicator>true</cbc:ChargeIndicator>
    <cbc:AllowanceChargeReason>Packing cost</cbc:AllowanceChargeReason>
    <cbc:Amount currencyID="KHR">10000</cbc:Amount>
</cac:AllowanceCharge>
<cac:AllowanceCharge>
    <cbc:ChargeIndicator>false</cbc:ChargeIndicator>
    <cbc:AllowanceChargeReason>Promotion discount</cbc:AllowanceChargeReason>
    <cbc:Amount currencyID="KHR">20000</cbc:Amount>
</cac:AllowanceCharge>
```
