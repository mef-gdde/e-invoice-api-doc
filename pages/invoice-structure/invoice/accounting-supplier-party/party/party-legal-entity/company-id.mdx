# cbc:CompanyID

An identifier for the party as registered within a company registration scheme. In our case, this would be the Ministry of Commerce (MOC) Single ID.

## About

- Occurrence: [0..1]
- Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonBasicComponents-2
