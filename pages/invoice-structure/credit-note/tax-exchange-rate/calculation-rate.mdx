
# cbc:CalculationRate

The factor/rate applied to the source currency to calculate the target currency. The rate must be taken from the National Bank of Cambodia (NBC).

- Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonBasicComponents-2
