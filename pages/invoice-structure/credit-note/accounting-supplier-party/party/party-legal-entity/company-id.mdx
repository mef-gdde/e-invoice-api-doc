# cbc:CompanyID

An identifier for the party as registered within a company registration scheme. In our case, this would be the Ministry of Commerce (MOC) Single ID. In the case the party is an end-consumer or organization without this ID, please put 0000.

## About

- Occurrence: [1..1]
- Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonBasicComponents-2

## Example

<cbc:CompanyID>000001234</cbc:CompanyID>
