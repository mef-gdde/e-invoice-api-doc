# cac:PartyLegalEntity
Details of the legal entity associated with the party.

## About
* Occurrence: [1..1]
* Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonAggregateComponents-2

## Child Elements

| Data Field                                  | Occurrence | Definition |
|---------------------------------------------|:----------:|------------|
| [cbc:RegistrationName](./RegistrationName.mdx)           |   [1..1]   | The name of the party as registered with the relevant legal authority. |
| [cbc:CompanyID](./CompanyID.mdx)                        |   [1..1]   | An identifier for the party as registered within a company registration scheme. If there is none, please put 0000. |
