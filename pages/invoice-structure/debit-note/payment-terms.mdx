# cac:PaymentTerms
Represents the terms and conditions of a payment agreement between the buyer and the seller.

## About
* Occurrence: [0..1]
* Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonAggregateComponents-2

## Child Elements

| Data Field                          | Occurrence | Definition |
|-------------------------------------|:----------:|------------|
| [cbc:Note](./payment-terms/note.mdx) |   [0..1]   | A textual description of the payment terms. |

## Example
```xml
<cac:PaymentTerms>
    <cbc:Note>Payment due within 30 days from the invoice date.</cbc:Note>
</cac:PaymentTerms>
