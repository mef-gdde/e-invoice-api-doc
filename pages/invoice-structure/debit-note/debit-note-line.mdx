# cac:InvoiceLine
A specific line of an invoice that describes a product or service.

## About
* Occurrence: [1..n]
* Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonAggregateComponents-2

## Child Elements

| Data Field                                      | Occurrence | Definition |
|-------------------------------------------------|:----------:|------------|
| [cbc:ID](./debit-note-line/id.mdx)                              |   [1..1]   | An identifier for this invoice line. |
| [cbc:DebitedQuantity](./debit-note-line/debited-quantity.mdx)  |   [1..1]   | The quantity (of items) on this debit note line. |
| [cbc:LineExtensionAmount](./debit-note-line/line-extension-amount.mdx) | [1..1] | The total amount for this invoice line, including allowance charges but net of taxes. |
| [cac:AllowanceCharge](./debit-note-line/allowance-charge.mdx)    |   [0..*]   | An allowance or charge associated with this invoice line. |
| [cac:TaxTotal](./debit-note-line/tax-total.mdx)                  |   [0..1]   | A total amount of taxes of a particular kind applicable to this invoice line. |
| [cac:Item](./debit-note-line/item.mdx)                          |   [1..1]   | The item associated with this invoice line. |
| [cac:Price](./debit-note-line/price.mdx)                        |   [0..1]   | The price of the item associated with this invoice line. |
