# UBL Invoice
The following table outlines the fields included in a UBL (Universal Business Language) invoice, their occurrence, their examples, and their definitions.
## Core Invoice Fields Definition
| Data Field            | Occurrence |                        Definition |
|-----------------------|:------------:|-----------------------------------|
|[cbc:UBLVersionID](./fields/id.mdx)| [0..1] | The version number for the UBL document. |
|[cbc:ID](/test) |	[1..1] |	Unique identifier for the invoice.|
|[cbc:DocumentCurrencyCode](/test) |	[1..1]	|The currency in which the invoice is denominated.
|[cac:AccountingSupplierParty](/test) |	[1..1] |	Party supplying the goods or services in the invoice.
|[cac:AccountingCustomerParty](/test) |	[1..1] |	Party to whom the goods or services are supplied.
|[cac:PrepaidPayment](/test) |	[0..n] |	Prepaid payment(s) applied to the invoice.
|[cac:TaxExchangeRate](/test) |	[0..1] |	Exchange rate applied for tax calculation in invoices involving conversion.
|[cac:TaxTotal](/test) |	[1..n] |	Total tax amount for the invoice.
|[cac:LegalMonetaryTotal](/test) | [1..1] |	Total amounts for the invoice.
|[cac:InvoiceLine](/test) |	[1..n] |	Each individual line item of the invoice.
|[cbc:IssueDate](/test) |	[1..1] |	Invoice issue Date.
|[cbc:InvoiceTypeCode](/test) |	[1..1] |	Type of code of invoice.
|[cbc:DocumentCurrencyCode](/test) |[1..1] |	Currency of the invoice amounts.
|[cbc:TaxAmount](/test) |[1..n]|	Tax amount for a specific tax category.
|[cbc:LineExtensionAmount](/test) |[1..n]|	Amount for a line item, excluding taxes.
|[cbc:TaxExclusiveAmount](/test)	|[1..1]|	Amount excluding taxes.
|[cbc:TaxInclusiveAmount](/test) |[1..1]|	Amount including taxes.
|[cbc:PayableAmount](/test) |[1..1]| Total amount payable.
