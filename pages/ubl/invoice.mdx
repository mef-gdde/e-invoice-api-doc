# UBL Invoice
# Invoice Field Definition
| Field                 | Occuring |                         Defintion |
|-----------------------|----------|-----------------------------------|
|cbc:UBLVersionID       |1-1       | Version of UBL                    |
|cbc:IssueDate          |1-1       | Invoice issue Date                |
|cbc:InvoiceTypeCode    | 1-1      | Type of code of invoice           |
