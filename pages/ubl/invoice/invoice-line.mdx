# cac:InvoiceLine
A specific line of an invoice that describes a product or service included in the invoice.

## About
* Occurrence: [1..n]
* Namespace: urn:oasis:names:specification:ubl:schema:xsd:CommonAggregateComponents-2

## Child elements

| Data Field                                      | Occurrence | Definition |
|-------------------------------------------------|:----------:|------------|
| [cbc:ID](./ID.mdx)                              |   [1..1]   | An identifier for this invoice line. |
| [cbc:InvoicedQuantity](./InvoicedQuantity.mdx)  |   [0..1]   | The quantity (of items) on this invoice line. |
| [cbc:LineExtensionAmount](./LineExtensionAmount.mdx) | [1..1] | The total amount for this invoice line, including allowance charges but net of taxes. |
| [cac:AllowanceCharge](./AllowanceCharge.mdx)    |   [0..*]   | An allowance or charge associated with this invoice line. |
| [cac:TaxTotal](./TaxTotal.mdx)                  |   [0..*]   | A total amount of taxes of a particular kind applicable to this invoice line. |
| [cac:Item](./Item.mdx)                          |   [1..1]   | The item associated with this invoice line. |
| [cac:Price](./Price.mdx)                        |   [0..1]   | The price of the item associated with this invoice line. |
